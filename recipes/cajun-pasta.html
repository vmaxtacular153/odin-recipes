This One Pot Creamy Cajun Chicken Pasta is incredibly easy to make, so it is perfect for weeknight dinners. I have a few variations for you below so be sure to read on!

Ingredients

  • 1 Tbsp olive oil
  • 1 Tbsp butter
  • 1 lb boneless, skinless chicken breast
  • 1 yellow onion, diced
  • 1/2 lb penne pasta
  • 15 oz roasted diced tomatoes
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 2 oz cream cheese
  • 2 Tbsp cajun seasoning
  • 3 green onion, sliced

Directions

  1. Cut chicken into cubes and combine with Cajun seasoning.
  2. Add oil and butter to a skillet on medium heat. Once hot, add chicken and sear.
  3. Add diced yellow onion and cook until translucent.
  4. Next, add pasta, tomatoes with juices and chicken stock to pan. Bring to a boil
  5. Bring down to a simmer and cook for about 10 minutes, or until pasta is cooked and sauce has thickened.
  6. Remove from heat and add cream cheese until mixed through. Top with green onions and serve.
